The House

17 Aug 2019

There has been a dwelling on this site since the 15th century and although it is now essentially a family home that has been added to and renovated over the years, there is a feeling of permanence and grounding within its walls.

Set in 10 acres of North Norfolk countryside, on the edge of a village and within striking distance of the sea, it encompasses a poplar wood, fledgling wild flower meadow and a kitchen garden. There are 6 double bedrooms available for guests, spread across the main house and a converted barn just across the drive. 4 of the rooms have private or en suite bathrooms, and 2 share a spacious bathroom and shower. In addition, there is an outdoor, heated swimming pool, hot tub, sauna and well equipped gym. Summer evenings are often spent in the garden - but when the nights close in, there is always the option of a movie in the cinema, or curling up in front of a log fire with a good book. It really can be very hard to leave!
